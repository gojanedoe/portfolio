Debug.Log

Made with: Unity (C#)
Roles: Programmer, Game Designer

Debug.Log_ is a cooperative hold-out “horde-style” game where two players fight off increasing waves of enemies for an allotted amount of time. An individual scoring system establishes competition, while the lose condition being tied to either player's death enforces a strict partnership. Communication and coordination are key to survival.

This game was made in collaboration with Isaac Buckley, Dylan Burrell, and Ben Price.
