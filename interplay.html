Interplay

Made with: Processing 3.0, Spacebrew, Kinect v1, and projector

Interplay is an interactive installation that explores boundaries and play within digital and physical spaces. Using a laptop that controls the shape and movement of a projected circle, players may interact with other participants remotely by changing their projected environment.

Participants under the projection must stay inside the circle for it to stay green, creating incentive for them to expand their personal boundaries.
