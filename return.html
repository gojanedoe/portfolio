Return

Made with: Unity C#
Roles: Programming, Game Design

Return is a single-player, puzzle platformer game where the player assumes the role of a wanderer seeking to bring a loved one back from the realm of the dead.

During gameplay, the character's lantern allows them to shift in and out of the spirit realm for a short time, giving them the ability to manipulate wisps, interact with sacred totems, and bypass unnatural man-made objects. Using the lantern, the player must solve puzzles and light various torches to open the way toward the one they're searching for.

This game was created in collaboration with Isaac Buckley, Nadine Elsigai, Lci Iarocci.
