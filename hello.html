Hello

Made with: HTML, CSS
Roles: Research, Writing, Web Design and Development

Hello is a net art-inspired website created in collaboration with Isaac Buckley. The website is currently deactivated, but it was previously hosted at www.initiateweb.world.

The site includes text, images, gifs, and hyperlinks as the main source of visuals and interaction. The site narrator is an entity formed solely though information gathered on the internet. It struggles with opposing viewpoints and contradictory aspects that exist on the web, and uses text and images as a way to convey its observations and thoughts.
