Storylines

Made with: Unity (C#)
Roles: Game Designer, Writer

Storylines is a social, local multiplayer mobile game for 4-30 players where players compete to become the best storyteller. The game is driven by a mobile app, but the main interaction takes place between players who are sharing stories face to face.

This game was developed by the MICA Game Lab in partnership with Art Works for Change, partly based on their previous project Aware/Oware. The development team included Isaac Buckley, Jason Corace, Lci Iarocci, and Will Pyle.

Our main goal with this game was to create a unique experience that enables players to share, discuss and empathize with stories of empowerment.
